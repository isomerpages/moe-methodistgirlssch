Netball is one of the increasingly popular and established team sports that is offered in MGS Primary. Through an array of curated modified games, the girls learn the fundamental skills like ball-handling, throwing and catching and the various locomotor skills that are essential in netball in a fun and positive environment. This fast-paced CCA creates a platform for the players to develop self-discipline, resilience, teamwork and sportsmanship while they hone their netball skills. While fostering the passion for the sport, the players will excel in their stride and forge lifelong friendship.
   
**Achievements:**

*   SPSSC National School Games Netball Competition 2022, West Zone Senior Division - Champion
    
*   SPSSC National School Games Netball Competition 2022, West Zone Junior Division - 1st Runner-up
