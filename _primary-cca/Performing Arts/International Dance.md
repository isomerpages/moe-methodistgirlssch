International Dance aims to develop dancers with discipline, determination and excellence. Through the practice sessions, the dance group builds camaraderie as they work together and learn to embrace the different strengths that each dancer brings to the team. No dancer will be left behind with the strong support of  the dance group.

*   Values and competencies the CCA aims to develop: Discipline, determination and excellence
