Rhythmic Gymnastics is a performance-based sport that revolves around gymnastics, dance, flexibility, grace and hand-eye coordination. Our gymnasts train twice a week. They go through a vigorous training schedule to perfect their routines and for fitness conditioning. Pupils learn about sportsmanship, teamwork, fair play and dedication through the mastery of fundamental skills. They are constantly encouraged to strive for excellence and put their best effort into anything they do.
