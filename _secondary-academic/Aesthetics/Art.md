### Aims and Objectives

The Art Department in MGS aims to build confident art learners who communicate visually to create meaning and connections with the society and culture. The programme delivers a future-oriented art curriculum to develop art learnersâ€™ competencies and appreciation for art as they perceive the world with increased awareness and aesthetic sensitivity.

### Curriculum

**Big Ideas**

Art helps us to see in new ways  <br>
Art tells stories about our world  <br>
Art influences how we live  
  

**Secondary 1 / Year 1**

Self and the Environment  
* Pen and Pencil  
* Watercolour  
* Printmaking

**Secondary 2 / Year 2**

People and the Community  
* Art Writing  
* 2D Design  
* Painting  
* 3D Design  
  

**Secondary 3 / Year 3**

* Art Research & Inquiry  
* Studio Practice  
* Art Writing  
* Art Synectics  
* Art Problem Solving

* Art Research & Inquiry  
* Art Synectics

* Technical Processes  
* Composition & Layout  
* Personal Responses

Comprises individual and group term projects and time-based examinations
