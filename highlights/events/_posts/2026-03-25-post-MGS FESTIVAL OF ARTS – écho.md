<p>Methodist Girls’ School (Secondary) proudly presents Festival of Arts
(FOA) 2026 - écho. écho embodies the reverberations of our shared repertoire
of music, dance, and drama. It is a showcase of our passion to create change
through the performing arts, where every note is a spark, every movement
a message, and every story a bridge that links people from all walks of
life and generations. écho sets the stage for everyone to enjoy the arts,
building ripple effects that embrace diverse voices, shared experiences,
and a sense of community. Our partners include seniors from Intergenerational
Learning, peers from other educational institutions, friends with diverse
abilities, and social welfare services. From the first note to the final
bow, discover how the performing arts can bridge the ages and ignite a
wave of change. Join us as we transform individual whispers into resounding
waves, proving that every voice, regardless of age or talent, can start
a ripple that changes the world.</p>
