Modern Jazz is a blend of street jazz, classical ballet and theatre dance, with its mixed roots extending back to both African and European traditions. It is a dance style that encompasses rhythm, explosive techniques and showmanship.

During each CCA session, students explore a myriad of unique movements, elaborate footwork and choreography that challenge them to go beyond their comfort zone.

*   Values and competencies the CCA aims to develop  
    

*   Team spirit
    
*   Mutual respect
    
*   Appreciation and consideration for others
