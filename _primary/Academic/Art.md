The Art programme in MGS aims to cultivate in every girl a lifelong enjoyment and appreciation of the visual arts through exposure to a variety of art media, artists and artworks. Every girl acquires knowledge, skills and values that equip her to:

*   Observe, analyse and reflect on her visual experiences
    
*   Express, create and communicate confidently to achieve her artistic intentions
    
*   Connect with and respond to the environment and cultures around her
    
*   Understand and appreciate the value of art in her life and in society
    

The learning objectives, outcomes and approaches of the Art programme in MGS are closely aligned with that of the MOE Art Syllabus. More details can be found at the following [website](https://www.moe.gov.sg/primary/curriculum/syllabus).

### Assessment

  

Portfolio Assessment is conducted once a semester.<br>
Final grade is derived from the mean grade of all art pieces completed during each semester.
