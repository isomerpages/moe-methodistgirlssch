Softball is one of the three team sports offered in MGS Primary. It is a sport that requires adaptability, speed, agility and teamwork. In addition to developing basic sports skills and competencies, our CCA aims to develop the values of self-discipline, resilience and sportsmanship in our young players. Friendly matches are incorporated in the training programme to provide a fun and real game situation. Softball camp is also held at the end of the year to allow players to build stronger bonds and reflect on the year. Players also set personal and team goals for the following year.Â
