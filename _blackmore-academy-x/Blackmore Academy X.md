<p><strong><a href="https://blackmoreacademyx.seemeconnect.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Blackmore Academy X</a></strong> is
a curated platform that supports the personalisation of learning and enrichment
at Methodist Girls’ School. It brings together opportunities beyond the
core curriculum — including events, workshops, learning journeys, and enrichment
experiences — while also highlighting student achievements and engagement
with the wider community. Anchored in the spirit of <strong>MGLearns</strong> and <strong>MGServes</strong>,
Blackmore Academy X helps students discover and develop their interests,
extend their strengths, and grow as confident, principled, and future-ready
learners.</p>
