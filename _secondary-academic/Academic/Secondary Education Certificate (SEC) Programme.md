<p>In the SEC track, we prepare students not just for the Singapore-Cambridge
Secondary Education Certificate (SEC) examinations, but for a purposeful
future. Beyond academic knowledge, we seek to equip our students with lifelong
competencies and dispositions that enable them to adapt and thrive in an
increasingly complex and changing world. Our students are encouraged to
cultivate humility in learning, curiosity about the world, resilience in
the face of challenges, and a contributive spirit towards others and society.</p>
<p>Through meaningful learning experiences and exposure beyond the classroom,
students widen their perspectives and develop a deeper understanding of
the world around them. Opportunities such as the Work Experience programme
allow students to gain first-hand insights into the workplace, helping
them connect classroom learning with real-world applications and explore
possible future pathways. Ultimately, our goal is for every MGS girl to
discover and pursue her interests and passions, anchored in an enduring
sense of purpose.</p>
<h4><br>Lower Secondary</h4>
<p>In the lower secondary years, students build strong foundations through
a broad-based curriculum. Core academic subjects include:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>English Language</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Mother Tongue Language (Chinese / Malay / Tamil)*</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Mathematics</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Science</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Geography</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>History</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Literature in English
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Students also take common curriculum subjects such as Art, Music, Physical
Education, Food &amp; Consumer Education, Project Work, Lower Sec Computing,
Contemporary Issues: Perspectives &amp; Inquiry (CPI), as well as Character
&amp; Citizenship Education (CCE), as part of a holistic education.</p>
<p>*Students with strong aptitude may offer Higher Mother Tongue Language
(HMTL).
<br>
</p>
<h4><br>Upper Secondary</h4>
<p>In the upper secondary years, students work towards the <strong>Singapore-Cambridge Secondary Education Certificate (SEC)</strong>.
They typically offer a combination of subjects based on their strengths
and interests.</p>
<p>All students offer the following core subjects:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>English Language</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Mother Tongue Language (or Higher Mother Tongue)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Mathematics</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Humanities (comprising Social Studies and choice of one elective in Geography,
History or Literature in English)</p>
</li>
</ul>
